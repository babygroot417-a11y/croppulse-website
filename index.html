Smart Drone-based Crop Advisory System for Small & Marginal Farmers

Learn More

🚨 The Problem

Small farmers struggle with late pest detection, improper water & fertilizer usage, and lack of access to affordable crop advisory systems. This leads to low yields and high input costs.

✅ Our Solution

CropPulse uses a rule-based drone algorithm to scan fields and provide block-level analysis. With sensors for pH, soil fertility, moisture, temperature, chlorophyll, and pests, our system generates accurate crop advisories. Farmers receive results through a multilingual mobile app with voice support.

🌟 Key Features

  • Drone scanning with grid/block-based analysis
  • Rule-based algorithm (no AI dependency)
  • Color-coded zones (Green / Yellow / Red)
  • Multilingual mobile app (English, Hindi, Telugu)
  • Voice chatbot for low-literate farmers
  • Weather-based alerts & predictive insights
